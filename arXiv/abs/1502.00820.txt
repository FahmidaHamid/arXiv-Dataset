  This study examines features such as the population structure, growth and
reproduction of the pearl mullet captured in the Lake Ercek. Between the dates
January 2008 and January 2010 a total number of 527 individual were sampled
through using trammel net and mesh size of 20,22 and 24 mm of fish net. The
fork length, total and gonad weight, sex and fecundity of the pearl mullet
which were captured has been identified; their age determination has been done
through their operculum and otolith; their condition factor and gonadosomatic
index value has been estimated.
